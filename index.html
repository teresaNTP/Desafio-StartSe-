London

Discovery London

London is one of the most popular cities in the world. It’s home to charming pubs, world-class (and often free) museums, tons of history, some of the best theater in the world, a diverse population, incredible food, and a wild nightlife.


Vibrant, modern, elegant, busy, rich in culture and entertainment options. Features like these, among many others, make it clear why London is one of the most sought after destinations in the world. The capital of England and the United Kingdom is also the seat of the British monarchy, a city that is home to Big Ben and many other famous postcards, such as the Tower of London Bridge, which is on the banks of the River Thames; Buckingham Palace; Westminster Abbey, as well as several very interesting museums.


The official language is English and the tourist who masters this "universal language" will have no difficulty, even if the elegant British accent is a little difficult to understand at first. However, like any self-respecting cosmopolitan city, London brings together people from all over the world; including a huge amount of Brazilians, locals or just tourists. The local currency is the pound sterling (Pound sterling), considered one of the strongest economically (beating the dollar and the euro). And even if at first glance everything seems too expensive, know that it is possible to haggle and find good deals.

Greece

Discovery Greece

Discover findings from the Prehistoric and Archaic Periods, unique works from Classical, Hellenistic, Medieval and Byzantine monuments, as well as creations from folk art cultures, different civilisations and religions, coexisting with current creations, modern and contemporary art.


Greece, known to the Greeks as Hellas, is considered the birthplace of music, philosophy and also theater. The Olympic Games were also born there and were held in the city of Olympia in 776 BC. It was in Ancient Greece, in the Classical Period, that the Greeks cultivated beauty, architecture, democracy and sculptures. Athens is the capital of the country and an outdoor spectacle, especially when walking close to the Acropolis and viewing the Parthenon, the biggest postcard of the place and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Brazil

Discovery Brazil

With its unique cuisine, rich culture, some of the most beautiful beaches in the world, breathtaking waterfalls, and adventure for everyone’s liking, all you have to do is choose the perfect type of tourism for you and be amazed by the wonders of Brazil.


One of the world's most captivating places, Brazil is a country of powdery white-sand beaches, verdant rainforests and wild, rhythm-filled metropolises, with its attractions extending to otherworldly landscapes of red-rock canyons, thundering waterfalls and coral-fringed tropical islands.


Then there's Brazil's biodiversity: legendary in scope, its diverse ecosystems boast the greatest collection of plant and animal species found anywhere on earth. There are countless places where you can spot iconic species in Brazil, including toucans, scarlet macaws, howler monkeys, capybara, pink dolphins, sea turtles and thousands of other living species.
